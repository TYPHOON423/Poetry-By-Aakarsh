Only You

There was one
who had me won.
The greatest of all
It was her, but none.

Her brown eyes,
That had me sinking
everytime I looked into them.
And ahh, the pretty face that made me drunk.

There was a smile,
That had me stuck for quite a while.
And then there was me
Nowhere near her, was all I could see.

Thank God she wasn't a judge
I was accepted, the way I was.
As our friendship grew,
There were some who couldn't see us.

And then came the day,
A line between us drew its way.
For the sake of fading the line away
I had to say,"Ay, it's only you, but none."

-Aakarsh Pratyush
